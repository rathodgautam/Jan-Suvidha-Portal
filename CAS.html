Coaching Assistance Scheme


What is Coaching Assistance Scheme?

Coaching Assistance Scheme for Pre-preparation of Recruitment Exams for Scheduled Caste Students.

Scheduled Caste Students of Class-1, 2 and 3 taken by UPSC / GPSC / State Commission / Bank / LIC / Secondary Service Selection Board / District Panchayat Selection Board A maximum of Rs. Assistance up to Rs. 20,000 /- is provided.


Eligibility Criteria:

  • Must have passed the graduation examination with 50% or more marks.
  • The maximum age limit will be 5 years for males and 20 years for females.
  • Neither the applicant nor the applicant's parents have a government job.
  • The institution in which the student wants to study should be as follows.
  • The organization should have more than three years of experience.

Required Documents:

  • Student Aadhaar card
  • Example of applicant's caste / sub-caste
  • Certificates in which the percentage of calculated percentage of the last marksheet passed
  • Proof of Residence (Electricity Bill / License / Lease Agreement / Election Card / Ration Card any one of them)
  • Certificate of income
  • Copy of first page of bank passbook / canceled check (name of applicant)
  • Photo showing the board of the institution in which admission is to be taken
  • The prescribed certificate of the institution in which you want to study
