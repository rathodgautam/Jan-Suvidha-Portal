Digital Gujarat


What is digital Gujarat?

Digital Gujarat Scholarship is a scholarship scheme launched by the government of Gujarat to provide financial assistance to the students of the state of Gujarat. The Digital Gujarat Scholarship is available for students studying from class 1 to post-graduation.

The aim of the Digital Gujarat Scholarship is to provide financial assistance to the students and help the students choose the career path they wish to without any financial problems.
